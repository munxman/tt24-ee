Occupational health tools

Six practical tools. Each one runs entirely in your browser; no data is shared with third parties.

01 / Compliance

Compliance checklist

All core occupational health obligations in one list. Tick off what you have done and see at a glance what is still outstanding.

Open → 02 / Budget

Cost calculator

Enter your headcount and get an indicative price for occupational health services. Compares the cost of the service against the potential fine.

Open → 03 / Assessment

Self-assessmentStart here - 2 minutes

Ten questions, instant result. We show you where you are strong and what needs attention.

Open → 04 / Schedule

Deadlines schedule

Which obligations fall due and when. Health surveillance deadlines, risk assessment reviews, and recurring tasks.

Open → 05 / Documents

Documents and templates

Ten print-ready templates. Risk assessment, notification record, first-aid register, health surveillance referral letter.

Open → 06 / Tax-free benefits

Health benefit

Employers may reimburse employees' health-promotion expenses up to a certain amount free of tax. The calculator shows how much of your allowance remains unused.
